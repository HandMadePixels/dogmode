Sanitization

In order to ensure the cleanest facility possible, our indoor areas are cleaned 2 x daily by a floor cleaning machine that applies 79 pounds of scrubbing pressure. This machine applies disinfectant, sucks it back up and then blows the floor dry. This process leaves the floor cleaner and dryer for all the dogs to enjoy!

Each morning, personal bedding is collected and washed in our commercial size machines if needed. Every sleeping quarter in the building is then sprayed down and sanitized to prepare for the next night's stay.

All feeding trays and watering bowls are cleaned after each meal.
